    2023-24 BOE Meeting Calendar [Adobe PDF] - Updated February 12, 2024

     

    Meetings: The Board of Education holds its regular meetings once a month on Monday at 6 p.m. Please see the BOE Meeting Calendar below for dates. Regular Board meetings are held at the North High Lecture Hall, 1437 Rochester, in the historic midtown area of Wichita. The Board also hosts Committees of the Whole, 99% Awards for students, and Good Apple Awards for staff at various dates noted on the meeting calendar. Guest parking is available in the student/staff lot east of Rochester or along the street. Handicapped parking is available in the south parking lot. Please enter at the Tower entrance on the east side of North High School or the south entrance by the cafeteria. District staff and security officers are available to provide assistance.

     


    Where to watch BOE meetings: BOE regular meetings are telecast on Cox Cable Channel 20 and online at WPS-TV.

     

    Following each BOE regular meeting, broadcasts are scheduled at:

     

    • Tuesdays at 12:30 and 6:30 p.m.
    • Wednesdays at 2 a.m., 8 a.m. and 6:30 p.m.
    • Fridays at 2 a.m.
    • Saturdays at 2 a.m. and 2 p.m.
    • Sundays at 2 a.m. and 2 p.m.

     

    Special BOE meetings may also be broadcast.

     

    Select BOE meeting discussions, including Good News and budget discussions, are available on YouTube.

     

    Download note: To download the Adobe PDF in any browser, right-click on the link and select the "Save Target As" or "Save Link As" option depending on the browser. Save the Adobe PDF file to your device, then open the PDF using Adobe Acrobat DC or other Adobe Acrobat products.


    For more information, please call the Clerk of the Board's office at 973-4553.
